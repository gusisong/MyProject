第二页 PPT 讲稿
  • 在 BKM 会议原方案一的基础上，我们进一步落地了两项降本优化措施 [cite: 35]：
    • 一是优化了空料架的回流路径，调整为由仪征直送上海港，单台节约成本 0.3 元 [cite: 33]。
    • 二是依托全程直达运输，取消了安亭仓库的入库与中转操作，单台进一步降本 0.64 元 [cite: 33]。
  • 在运输节奏上我们也进行了同步优化：通过充分利用港口 4 天的免堆存期以及一阶优惠费率，实行分批次均衡发运，每日发运量控制在 2 至 4 个集装箱 [cite: 22, 33, 222, 227]。
  • 按五月份首批进口量测算，仪征工厂仓库的峰值占用面积将由 246 平方米缩减至 141 平方米，降幅达 43%，有效缓解了工厂的库存周转压力 [cite: 33]。
  • 针对方案一潜在的属地商检查验风险，我们已提前与海关对接确认 [cite: 33]。属地海关将在货物到厂后 3 个工作日内完成上门查验，这完全能够保障产线的正常运转，消除断供隐患 [cite: 33]。
  • 综上所述，经过对运输路径、仓储及操作节奏的全方位优化，相较于 BKM 会议的初始版本，方案一的单台整体成本进一步下降了 0.94 元，实现了降本、提效与稳供应的多重收益 [cite: 33, 35]。

  • The recent CW03 2026 Special BKM Meeting has approved in principle the overall plan to implement the Japanese-spec AQ160 transmission [cite: 9].
  • During the meeting, we evaluated two inland logistics solutions from Shanghai to Yizheng [cite: 9, 10]:
    • Concept 1 involves direct delivery from the Shanghai Port to the CPY plant [cite: 10].
    • Concept 2 utilizes the standard process, routing deliveries through the Anting warehouse [cite: 10, 24, 25].
  • Based on our comprehensive economic assessment, we highly recommend proceeding with Concept 1, the direct delivery model [cite: 10, 21].
Page 2
  • Building upon the original Concept 1 presented at the BKM meeting, we have secured two additional cost-saving measures [cite: 35]:
    • First, we optimized the empty rack return route to a direct delivery from CPY to the Shanghai Port, yielding a saving of 0.3 RMB per set [cite: 33].
    • Second, by eliminating the Anting warehouse operations entirely, we achieved an additional cost reduction of 0.64 RMB per set [cite: 33].
  • Furthermore, we have optimized the delivery rhythm. By maximizing the port's 4-day free storage period and utilizing first-tier storage rates, we will implement a phased delivery approach, dispatching a controlled maximum of 2 to 4 containers per day [cite: 22, 33, 222, 227].
  • Based on the first batch in May, the peak storage area requirement will be reduced from 246 square meters to 141 square meters [cite: 33]. This represents a 43% reduction that significantly eases inventory pressure at CPY [cite: 33].
  • Finally, regarding the potential risk of local customs inspections under Concept 1, we have proactively confirmed with Customs that any local inspection will be completed within 3 working days [cite: 33]. This guarantees that our production lines remain fully operational without any disruption [cite: 33].
  • In conclusion, compared to the initial Concept 1, these measures reduce the overall cost by an additional 0.94 RMB per set, successfully optimizing both cost and operational efficiency while ensuring a stable supply [cite: 33, 35].
